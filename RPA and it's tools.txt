what is the RPA : RPA stands for Robotic Process Automation. It is a technology that uses software robots or "bots" to automate repetitive and rule-based tasks within business processes. RPA tools are software platforms or frameworks that provide the capabilities to design, develop, deploy, and manage these software robots.
